# Research Report: Coca-Cola Chief Marketing Officer (2025–2026)

---

## Summary

The current top marketing executive at The Coca-Cola Company is **Manuel "Manolo" Arroyo**. His title was elevated in early 2026 to reflect an expanded scope. He is confirmed on the official Coca-Cola leadership page and across multiple third-party sources.

---

## Key Findings

### Full Name
**Manuel "Manolo" Arroyo**

### Current Title (as of 2026)
**Executive Vice President and Chief Marketing and Customer Commercial Officer**, The Coca-Cola Company

> Prior title (2020–2026): Executive Vice President and Global Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)

### Title Change Details
- Effective around **March 31, 2026**, Arroyo's role was expanded as part of a broader leadership reshuffle ahead of a new CEO transition.
- Customer and Commercial Leadership responsibilities were transferred to him from CFO John Murphy.
- Sources: Coca-Cola official press release (investors.coca-colacompany.com), Marketing Week, Atlanta Journal-Constitution (AJC)

### Scope of Role
Per the official Coca-Cola leadership bio, Arroyo oversees:
- Four Global Category teams
- Integrated Marketing Experience (IMX): creative, media, digital marketing, design
- Customer and Commercial Leadership (newly added in 2026)

### Background
- Joined Coca-Cola in **2017** as President of the Iberia franchise unit
- Became Global CMO in **2020**
- Education: IMD (International Institute for Management Development)
